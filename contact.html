Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday through Friday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Saturday and Sunday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for new members. This gives you the opportunity to experience our facilities and services before committing to a membership.

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Our memberships are on a month-to-month basis with a 30-day cancellation notice. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

Do you provide towels and lockers?

Yes, we provide complimentary towels for all members. Lockers are available for daily use at no additional cost. Premium and Elite members also have the option to rent a permanent locker for a small monthly fee.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a spacious parking lot available for all members at no additional cost.
